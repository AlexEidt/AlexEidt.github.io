Side-Scroller

For my final project in EE 371: Design of Digital Circuits and Systems, my partner and I chose to implement a Side-Scroller Game on a Field Programmable Gate Array (FPGA) on an Altera DE1 SOC Board.

The game consists of the player which must avoid the enemies (diamonds) coming towards them from right to left across the screen. The player can move up and down by pressing the left and right mouse buttons. Every time the player survives a "wave" of enemies, their score gets incremented, which is displayed as hex numbers on three of the DE1's seven-segment displays. Every time the user survives a wave, a note is played via speakers to signal this. If the user is to collide with an enemy, their score will reset to zero and that same note will be played, just three times as long.
